How to Play

Welcome to GK Kings, a general knowledge game where you can test your knowledge in various categories!

Step 1

One player becomes the King and hosts the game session. The King doesn't play but manages the game.

Step 2

Aspiring players join the game session created by the King.

Step 3

The King sets the category (e.g., animals, cars) and the starting letter for answers (e.g., "K" for animals, "Z" for cars).

Step 4

Players submit their answers within the specified time limit, ensuring they match the category and starting letter provided by the King.

Step 5

The King assigns points for correct answers and manages the game flow.

Step 6

The player with the highest score becomes the new King and hosts the next game session.
